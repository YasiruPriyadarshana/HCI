Yale University Library

Library resumes lending videos from the Film Study Center

In welcome news for film study faculty and students, the library has resumed lending items from the Film Study Center’s large collection of videos. The collection contains nearly 40,000 DVDs, more than 4,000 Blu-ray discs and close to 6,000 VHS tapes.

Library resumes lending videos from the Film Study Center

Library to add evening hours beginning Oct. 7

Opening hours at Sterling Library and Bass Library have been extended to 10 pm Monday through Thursday. The two adjacent, connected libraries are now open 68 hours a week on the following schedule: Sunday, noon - 6 pm; Monday - Thursday, 10 am - 10 pm; Friday - Saturday, 10 am - 5 pm
